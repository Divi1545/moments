How We Keep Moments Safe

  • Automated Content Filtering: All moment titles and messages are automatically screened for inappropriate content, spam, and banned keywords.
  • User Reporting: Report any moment or message that violates our guidelines. Content is automatically hidden after 3 reports.
  • User Blocking: Block any user to prevent them from seeing your messages or joining the same moments as you.
  • Human Review: Our moderation team reviews flagged content and takes action within 24 hours.
  • Zero Tolerance: Harassment, hate speech, illegal content, and threats result in immediate account suspension.

üö© How to Report Content

Reporting a Moment

  1. Open the moment details page
  2. Tap the flag icon (üö©) in the top right
  3. Select a reason: Spam, Inappropriate, Harassment, or Other
  4. Submit your report

Reporting a Message

  1. In the chat, find the message you want to report
  2. Tap the "Report" button below the message
  3. Select a reason and submit

What happens next? Content that receives 3 or more reports is automatically hidden. Our team reviews all reports and may take additional action including warning users, suspending accounts, or contacting authorities for illegal content.

üö´ Blocking Users

If someone is bothering you, you can block them:

  1. In a chat, tap "Block User" below their message
  2. Confirm the block

When you block someone:

  • They won't see your messages
  • You won't see their messages
  • They can't join moments you're in
  • You can't join moments they're in

Manage your blocked users in Settings.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long do moments last?

Moments can last anywhere from 1 hour to 24 hours. Once a moment expires, all messages and photos are automatically deleted.

Can I delete a message I sent?

Currently, messages cannot be deleted individually. However, all messages are automatically deleted when the moment expires.

What happens when I report content?

Your report is anonymous. Content that receives 3 or more reports is automatically hidden. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

How do I unblock someone?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Blocked Users, then tap "Unblock" next to the user's name.

Is my location shared publicly?

No. We only show approximate locations for moments (within a few hundred meters). Your exact location is never shared with other users.

Can I host multiple moments at once?

No. You can only host one active moment at a time. However, you can join multiple moments hosted by others.
